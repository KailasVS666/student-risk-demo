About AI Student Mentor

Empowering educators with AI-driven early warning systems to identify at-risk students

Our Mission

Model Information

Dataset

Trained on the UCI Student Performance Dataset, comprising data from Portuguese secondary schools with 30+ features including demographics, academic history, and social factors.

Performance

Achieved 85%+ accuracy in predicting student risk levels. The model uses gradient boosting with optimized hyperparameters for balanced precision and recall.

Explainability

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values provide transparent, interpretable insights into which features most influence each prediction, ensuring trustworthy AI decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the risk prediction?

Our model achieves 85%+ accuracy on test data. However, predictions should be used as one tool among many in assessing student needs, not as the sole decision-maker.

What data is required for assessment?

The system requires student demographics (age, gender), family background (parental education, family size), academic history (past grades, failures), and behavioral factors (study time, absences).

Is student data secure?

Yes! All data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore with user authentication. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and only accessible by the authenticated user.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely! Visit the Settings page to export all your assessment data in JSON format at any time.

Is this system free to use?

Yes! This prototype uses free-tier services from Firebase (Spark Plan) and Google Gemini API, supporting up to 1,500 requests per day.
